How to Order

Getting your medical support products is easy! Follow our simple ordering process to receive the quality care products you need.

Simple 4-Step Process

Follow these easy steps to get your medical support products quickly and efficiently.

1

Download & Fill Form

Download our order form and complete all required fields including personal information, insurance details, and product needs.

2

Submit Your Order

Send your completed form via email, fax, or bring it to our Brooklyn location. Include any supporting documents or prescriptions.

3

Insurance Verification

We'll verify your insurance coverage and contact you to confirm details, answer questions, and schedule fitting if needed.

4

Receive Your Products

Pick up your custom-fitted products at our store or arrange for delivery. We'll ensure everything fits perfectly and comfortably.
